Welcome to VZTA Trails!


Explore the world around you in a whole new way with VZTA Trails - your gateway to a gamified trail experience!


As an admin, you have the power to craft unique trails by adding checkpoints that will challenge and engage users.

Each checkpoint corresponds to a real-world location, making the adventure both virtual and tangible.


Users can embark on these thrilling trails, discovering checkpoints and collecting them using QR codes.

It's a blend of virtual excitement and real-world exploration, making VZTA Trails the perfect platform for adventure seekers and trail enthusiasts.
